About SUT

Our mission, our people, and 55+ years of advancing underwater technology worldwide.

Our Story

55+ Years at the Frontier of the Ocean

The Society for Underwater Technology was founded in London in 1966 by engineers and scientists who recognised the enormous potential of working beneath the sea.

We operate as a learned society — all proceeds from events, memberships, and publications are reinvested into our educational programmes and community activities.
