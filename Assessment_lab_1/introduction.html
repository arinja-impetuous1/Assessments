Who We Are

Society for Underwater Technology

Founded in 1966, SUT is a multidisciplinary learned society bringing together individuals and organisations with a common interest in underwater technology, ocean science, and offshore engineering.

From subsea robotics and marine renewables to oceanography and diving technology, SUT spans the full breadth of activity in the underwater world.

More About Us

Our Mission

To advance the knowledge and understanding of underwater technology for the benefit of society, enabling a sustainable relationship with the world's oceans.
